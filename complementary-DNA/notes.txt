Complementary DNA

In DNA strings, symbols "A" and "T" are complements of each 
other, as "C" and "G". You have function with one side of the DNA 
you need to get the other complementary side. DNA strand is never empty 
or there is no DNA at all.

Rules:
A -> T
C -> G

Tests:
- letter gives back same letter
- A gives back T
- T gives back A
- C gives back G
- G gives back C
- AATT gives back TTAA
- CCGG gives back GGCC
- CAGT gives back GTCA
- CVDTASG gives back GVDATSC
